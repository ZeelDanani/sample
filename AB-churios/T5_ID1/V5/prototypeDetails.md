# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Parents of babies and toddlers (ages 6 months to 3 years) seeking convenient, nutritious, and engaging snack options delivered directly to their home.

---

## Value Proposition

Churios Subscription Boxes offer parents a monthly delivery of curated Churios cereal varieties, tailored for babies and toddlers. Each box is ordered via a dedicated DTC website, with seamless social media integration for sharing unboxing moments and providing feedback. This service simplifies snack planning, introduces new flavors, and fosters a sense of community among parents. The model leverages subscription convenience and digital engagement to support parents' desire for healthy, fun, and hassle-free feeding experiences for their young children.

---

## Business Impact

Drives recurring revenue through direct-to-consumer subscriptions, increases brand engagement via social sharing, and strengthens loyalty among young families. The approach supports the focused goal of increasing brand relevance and preference among parents of babies.
