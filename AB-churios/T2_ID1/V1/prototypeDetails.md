# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Families with children aged 6–14 who enjoy hands-on kitchen activities and are active on social media platforms, seeking fun, interactive snack experiences they can share and customize at home.

---

## Value Proposition

Churios DIY Snack Kits provide families with all the ingredients and step-by-step instructions needed to create unique, Churios-based snacks at home. Each kit includes portioned Churios, creative mix-ins, and access to exclusive online recipe tutorials and unboxing content. The experience is designed to be interactive and shareable, encouraging families to bond over snack-making and to showcase their creations online. The kit's limited-edition nature drives excitement and urgency, while the unboxing and tutorial content supports easy, enjoyable execution for all skill levels.

---

## Business Impact

This initiative generates incremental revenue through premium kit sales, amplifies brand engagement via user-generated content, and strengthens Churios' relevance among families seeking experiential snacking. It leverages social sharing to organically expand reach and drive trial.
