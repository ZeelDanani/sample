# Marketing Brief

---

## Target Persona

Socially active Gen Z and Millennial consumers (ages 16–34) hosting or attending Super Bowl parties who frequently use Snapchat and Instagram to share real-time, interactive moments with friends.

---

## Value Proposition

General Mills will launch branded, interactive AR filters for Snapchat and Instagram, designed specifically for Super Bowl parties. These filters overlay custom ice cream animations and branded effects onto user photos and videos, making party moments more engaging and shareable. Users are encouraged to tag the brand when posting, amplifying organic reach. This approach directly integrates the product into social experiences, allowing users to showcase their celebrations while boosting real-time brand presence. The execution focuses on seamless digital activation, operational simplicity, and rapid deployment to coincide with Super Bowl festivities.

---

## Business Impact

The campaign drives immediate social engagement and brand awareness among a key demographic during a high-traffic event. By integrating branded filters into user-generated content, General Mills increases share-of-voice, stimulates demand for its ice cream products, and strengthens digital brand affinity in a competitive, event-driven market.
